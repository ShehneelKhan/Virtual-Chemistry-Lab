LAB 06

Apparatus

  • 1) Burette
  • 2) Pipette
  • 3) Conical flask
  • 4) Beakers
  • 5) Burette stand
  • 6) Funnel
  • 7) Indicator bottle

Method

  • • STEP 1: Place all the apparatus..
  • • STEP 2: Put glass rod on Bunsen burner and keeps rolling..
  • • STEP 3: On the Bunsen burner until the rod becomes soft.
  • • STEP 4: Now stretch the rod.

Precautions

  • 1) Note the lower meniscus of alkali solution in the burette.
  • 2) Remove air bubble from the jet of the burette.
  • 3) Rinse the burette and the pipette with NaOH and oxalic acid respectively.
  • 4) All the apparatus should be thoroughly washed.
  • 5) Only 1 or 2 drops of indicator should be used.
  • 6) The pipette should not be down to dropout the solution.
  • 7) The lower meniscus of the solution should be read.
  • 8) The pipette should not be blown to dropout the solution.
  • 9) The flask should be shaken after each addition of solution.
