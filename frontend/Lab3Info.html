LAB 03

Apparatus

  • 1) Beaker
  • 2) Thermometer
  • 3) Test tube
  • 4) Capillary tube
  • 5) Tripod stand
  • 6) Wire gauze
  • 7) Bunsen burner
  • 8) Stirrer
  • 9) Rubber band

Method

    Method

    FIND THE BOILING POINT OF ETHYL ALCOHOL

  • • STEP 1: Place all the apparatus .
  • • STEP 2: Attach the thermometer with a glass tube filled with Ethyl Alcohol and put a capillary tube inside a glass tube.
  • • STEP 3: On the Bunsen burner until it reaches its boiling point temperature and bubbles are coming from the capillary tube..
  • • STEP 4: Now stop the burner and note the temperature.

Precautions

  • 1) Water should be heated gently.
  • 2) Stirring should be constant.
  • 3) Thermometer bulb should not touch the walls and base of the beaker.
  • 4) Too much or too little sample can be cause of error.
  • 5) Adjust flame size so that the temperature rises no more than 2-3 degrees per minute.
  • 6) A sample is only used once for melting point detection.
  • 7) The graduated side of thermometer should face you.
